## 1. Print-on-Demand Partners (Zero Inventory -- Recommended to Start)

Print-on-demand (POD) means zero upfront inventory cost. You upload artwork, customers order, the provider prints and ships directly. You pay only when a sale is made.

### Tier 1: Best Overall

#### Printful

- **Website:** https://www.printful.com
- **Best for:** US, Canada, EU fulfillment
- **Products:** Art prints, canvas wraps, framed posters, phone cases, mugs, tote bags, t-shirts, hoodies, all-over-print apparel, stickers, pillows, blankets
- **Pricing examples (base cost to you):**
  - Poster print 12x18": ~$8.50
  - Canvas 12x16": ~$16.00
  - Unisex t-shirt (Bella+Canvas 3001): ~$9.50
  - Hoodie (Bella+Canvas 3719): ~$22.00
  - 11oz mug: ~$6.50
  - Phone case (iPhone, tough): ~$8.50
  - Tote bag: ~$10.00
- **Shipping times:**
  - US: 2-5 business days production + 3-5 days shipping
  - Canada: 2-5 production + 5-10 shipping
  - EU: 2-5 production + 3-8 shipping (EU fulfillment center)
  - India/Asia: 2-5 production + 10-20 shipping (ships from US/EU)
- **Branding options:** Inside labels, pack-ins, branded packing slips, custom packaging (for higher volume)
- **Integration:** Direct Shopify app, WooCommerce plugin, Etsy, Amazon, or API
- **Sign up:** Free account, no monthly fee, pay per order
- **Pros:**
  - Excellent print quality, especially for apparel and canvas
  - Built-in mockup generator saves time
  - Strong Shopify integration with automatic fulfillment
  - Warehousing available if you want to stock best-sellers later
  - Sample discounts (20% off)
- **Cons:**
  - Higher base prices than some competitors
  - International shipping from US/EU is slow and costly for Indian market
  - Limited facilities in Asia-Pacific region
- **Setup difficulty:** Easy (1-2 hours to connect store and list first products)

#### Gelato

- **Website:** https://www.gelato.com
- **Best for:** International fulfillment, especially India, UK, Australia
- **Products:** Art prints, canvas, phone cases, mugs, t-shirts, hoodies, tote bags, wall art, photo books, stationery
- **Pricing examples (base cost to you):**
  - Poster print 12x18": ~$5.50-8.00 (varies by local facility)
  - Canvas 12x16": ~$14.00
  - Unisex t-shirt: ~$8.50
  - 11oz mug: ~$5.50
  - Phone case: ~$7.50
- **Shipping times:**
  - Ships from the nearest facility to the customer
  - US: 2-4 production + 3-5 shipping
  - India: 2-4 production + 3-7 shipping (local Indian print partner)
  - UK: 2-4 production + 2-5 shipping
  - EU: 2-4 production + 3-5 shipping
  - Australia: 2-4 production + 3-6 shipping
- **Key advantage:** Print facilities in 30+ countries including India. Orders placed by Indian customers print locally in India, cutting shipping cost and time dramatically.
- **Integration:** Shopify, Etsy, WooCommerce, API
- **Sign up:** Free plan available; Gelato+ plan ($24/mo) gives better pricing and faster shipping
- **Pros:**
  - Best for Sikh diaspora market -- local production in India, UK, Canada, US, Australia
  - Lower international shipping costs than any competitor
  - Good environmental credentials (less cross-border shipping)
  - Competitive base pricing
- **Cons:**
  - Product quality can vary by local print partner
  - Fewer branding/customization options than Printful
  - Mockup generator less polished
- **Setup difficulty:** Easy (1-2 hours)
- **Recommendation:** Use Gelato as primary POD for international orders and Printful for US/Canada orders to optimize shipping cost and speed.

### Tier 2: Specialized

#### Gooten

- **Website:** https://www.gooten.com
- **Best for:** Competitive pricing, wide product catalog
- **Products:** Similar range to Printful plus home goods (blankets, shower curtains, coasters)
- **Pricing:** Generally 10-20% lower base cost than Printful on comparable products
- **Shipping:** US 3-7 business days, International 7-21 days
- **Integration:** Shopify, Etsy, WooCommerce, API
- **Pros:** Lower prices, large product catalog, good API
- **Cons:** Quality less consistent (uses network of vendors), less polish in dashboard
- **Setup difficulty:** Easy

#### Printify

- **Website:** https://www.printify.com
- **Best for:** Price comparison across multiple print providers
- **Products:** Apparel, home goods, accessories, art -- broadest catalog
- **Key feature:** Shows you multiple print providers for each product with different prices, shipping times, and quality ratings. You choose the provider per product.
- **Pricing:** Free plan (5 designs per day), Premium $29/mo (unlimited designs, up to 20% discount)
- **Shipping:** Varies by print provider selected
- **Integration:** Shopify, Etsy, WooCommerce, API, manual order
- **Pros:** Largest network, transparent pricing, you can compare providers
- **Cons:** Quality depends on chosen provider -- research ratings carefully
- **Setup difficulty:** Easy, but selecting optimal providers takes research time

#### SPOD (by Spreadshirt)

- **Website:** https://www.spod.com
- **Best for:** Fastest production time in the US and EU
- **Products:** Primarily apparel (t-shirts, hoodies, tanks), some accessories
- **Key feature:** 48-hour production guarantee (95% of orders ship within 48 hours)
- **Pricing:** Competitive with Printful; slightly lower on basics
- **Shipping:** US 2-5 days, EU 2-5 days after production
- **Integration:** Shopify, WooCommerce, API
- **Pros:** Speed, reliable quality for apparel
- **Cons:** Smaller product range, less suitable for art prints and home goods
- **Setup difficulty:** Easy

### POD Selection Summary

| Stage | Recommended Provider(s) | Why |
|-------|------------------------|-----|
| Starting out | Gelato + Printful | Gelato for global reach, Printful for US quality |
| Testing products | Printify | Compare providers, find best price/quality ratio |

## 2. Custom/Bulk Manufacturing (Once You Have Proven Sellers)

Move to bulk manufacturing when a product consistently sells 20+ units per month via POD. The upfront investment is higher but margins increase by 30-50%.

### Art Prints & Canvas

#### Bay Photo Lab (US -- Premium)

- **Website:** https://www.bayphoto.com
- **Best for:** Museum-quality prints, fine art reproductions, limited editions
- **Products:** Giclée prints, canvas gallery wraps, metal prints, acrylic prints, framed prints
- **Pricing:**
  - Giclée print 12x18": ~$12-18 (vs. POD ~$8.50, but significantly higher quality)
  - Canvas gallery wrap 16x20": ~$30-40
  - Metal print 12x18": ~$25-35
- **Minimum order:** No minimum -- order 1 or 100
- **Turnaround:** 2-5 business days
- **Why use them:** For signed limited edition prints, gallery-quality products that justify $60-200 retail pricing
- **Setup:** Create pro account, upload files, order. No API -- manual ordering.

#### Prodigi (UK/Global -- API-Driven)

- **Website:** https://www.prodigi.com
- **Best for:** Scaling print fulfillment with API, global reach
- **Products:** Prints, canvas, framed art, phone cases, mugs, apparel
- **Pricing:** Similar to Gelato; competitive for UK/EU/Asia
- **Key feature:** Full API with webhooks -- build automated fulfillment into your Shopify store
- **Minimum order:** No minimum (print on demand capable, but also bulk pricing)
- **Integration:** API, Shopify, Etsy, WooCommerce
- **Why use them:** When you outgrow basic POD and want more control over fulfillment logic

#### Local Print Shops

- **Best for:** Signed editions, event inventory, rush orders
- **Approach:** Find a local fine art printer. Many offer giclée printing.
- **Pricing:** Varies widely; expect $15-30 for a 12x18" giclée print
- **Advantage:** In-person quality checks, same-day turnaround possible, hand-sign prints before shipping
- **Tip:** Negotiate volume pricing -- 50+ prints at once can drop cost 20-30%

### Phone Cases

- **Products:** iPhone, Samsung, iPad cases -- slim, tough, and folio styles
- **Pricing:** $6-12 per unit at 25+ quantity

- **Turnaround:** 5-10 business days

#### Alibaba Suppliers (Bulk)

- **Pricing:** $1.50-4.00 per unit at 100+ quantity

## 3. Digital Products (100% Margin -- No Supplier Needed)

Digital products have zero cost of goods and infinite inventory. Build these from existing art assets.

### Product Ideas

| Product | Format | Suggested Price |
|---------|--------|----------------|
| Phone wallpapers (pack of 5-10) | JPEG/PNG, phone resolution | $3-5 |
| Desktop wallpapers (pack of 5-10) | JPEG/PNG, 4K resolution | $3-5 |
| High-res art downloads (individual) | JPEG/PNG, 300 DPI, print-ready | $5-15 |
| Art bundle (5-10 pieces) | ZIP file with multiple formats | $15-30 |
| WhatsApp sticker packs | WebP format, per WhatsApp specs | $2-4 |
| iMessage sticker packs | Distributed via Apple App Store | $1.99-3.99 |
| Coloring pages (digital) | PDF, printable at home | $3-8 per pack |
| Printable greeting cards | PDF with fold lines | $2-5 |
| Social media templates | Canva/PSD/PNG templates | $5-15 per pack |

### How to Deliver Digital Products

1. **Shopify Digital Downloads App** (free Shopify app)
   - Attach digital file to a product listing
   - Customer receives download link immediately after purchase
   - Simple, integrated with your store

2. **SendOwl** (https://www.sendowl.com) -- $9/mo
   - More advanced: drip delivery, PDF stamping, license keys
   - Good if you sell many digital products

3. **Gumroad** (https://www.gumroad.com) -- 10% fee per sale
   - Standalone digital product store
   - Built-in audience features, email marketing
   - Good for testing digital products without Shopify integration

4. **Email delivery** (manual)
   - For very early stage: customer buys, you email the file
   - Does not scale, but works for validating demand

### WhatsApp Sticker Packs -- Technical Details

- Format: WebP images, 512x512px
- File size: under 100KB per sticker
- Pack size: minimum 3, maximum 30 stickers
- Distribution: Build a simple Android/iOS app, or use third-party sticker maker apps
- Revenue model: Sell the pack via your store, provide download instructions

### iMessage Sticker Packs

- Requires Apple Developer Account ($99/year)
- Use Xcode to build a sticker pack app (no coding required for basic sticker packs)
- Submit to App Store -- standard review process
- Revenue: Apple takes 30% of the sale price

## 4. Children's Book Production

### Amazon KDP (Kindle Direct Publishing)

- **Website:** https://kdp.amazon.com
- **Best for:** Zero upfront cost, massive distribution, print-on-demand
- **Products:** Paperback and hardcover books
- **Cost:** $0 upfront. Amazon prints when a customer orders.
- **Pricing model:** You set the retail price. Amazon deducts printing cost + their cut.
  - Paperback printing cost: ~$2.50-4.00 for a 32-page color children's book (8.5x8.5")
  - Hardcover printing cost: ~$6.00-9.00
  - Amazon takes 40% of retail (expanded distribution) or keeps 30% margin (Amazon.com sales)
- **Royalty example:** Set retail at $16.99, printing cost $3.50, your royalty ~$3.40 per sale (Amazon.com)
- **Turnaround:** Books available on Amazon within 72 hours of approval
- **ISBN:** Free Amazon-assigned ISBN (but cannot be used elsewhere), or use your own
- **Pros:** Zero risk, global Amazon distribution, print on demand
- **Cons:** Amazon controls the customer relationship, lower margins than self-fulfillment

### IngramSpark

- **Website:** https://www.ingramspark.com
- **Best for:** Wider distribution (bookstores, libraries, online retailers beyond Amazon)
- **Cost:** $49 setup fee per title (fee sometimes waived during promotions)
- **Printing cost:** Similar to KDP -- $3-5 for a 32-page color children's book
- **Distribution:** 40,000+ retailers and libraries worldwide including Barnes & Noble, indie bookstores, international retailers
- **Discount model:** You set a trade discount (typically 55% for bookstores to stock your book)
- **Pros:** Professional distribution, returns program (bookstores require this), ISBN support
- **Cons:** Setup fee, lower margins with bookstore discounts, more complex dashboard

### Blurb

- **Website:** https://www.blurb.com
- **Best for:** Premium quality, small batch, portfolio-grade books
- **Printing cost:** Higher -- $15-30 for a full-color children's book
- **Minimum order:** 1 copy
- **Quality:** Superior paper stock, excellent color reproduction
- **Use case:** Premium signed copies, gift editions, collector's items
- **Retail pricing:** $30-50+ for premium editions

### Local Printer

- **Best for:** Signed copies with premium packaging for direct sales
- **Typical cost:** $5-15 per book at 50-100 copies
- **Advantage:** Custom packaging, hand-sign, ship yourself with branded materials
- **Tip:** Combine with POD for Amazon and local printer for signed/special editions

### ISBN Information

| Source | Cost | Notes |
|--------|------|-------|
| Amazon KDP (free) | $0 | Amazon-assigned, locked to Amazon distribution only |
| Bowker (US) | $125 for 1, $295 for 10 | You own the ISBN, use anywhere |
| IngramSpark | Included with setup | Ingram-assigned, usable beyond Ingram |
| Canadian ISBN Agency | Free (Canadian publishers) | For Canadian-registered businesses |

**Recommendation:** Buy a block of 10 ISBNs from Bowker ($295) if you plan multiple books. This gives you full control and professional credibility.

## 6. Step-by-Step Setup for Printful (Quick Start)

### Step 1: Create Your Free Account

- Go to https://www.printful.com and sign up
- No credit card required, no monthly fee
- Verify your email

### Step 2: Connect to Your Store

- In Printful dashboard, go to Stores > Add Store
- Select your platform (Shopify, Etsy, WooCommerce, etc.)
- Follow the authorization flow to connect
- For Shopify: Install the Printful app from the Shopify App Store

### Step 3: Upload Your Art Files

Recommended dimensions for key products:

| Product | Dimensions | Format |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| Poster (18x24") | 5400x7200px | PNG, 300 DPI |
| Canvas (16x20") | 4800x6000px + 450px edge wrap | PNG, 300 DPI |
| T-shirt | 4500x5400px | PNG, transparent BG, 300 DPI |
| Mug (11oz) | 2000x850px wraparound | PNG, 300 DPI |
| Phone case | Use Printful template (varies by model) | PNG, 300 DPI |
| Tote bag | 3600x4200px | PNG, transparent BG, 300 DPI |

### Step 4: Create Product Mockups

- Printful has a built-in mockup generator
- Upload your art, position it on the product
- Generate lifestyle mockups (model wearing shirt, mug on desk, etc.)
- Download mockups for your product listings

### Step 5: Set Retail Prices

Use the 2.5-3x cost rule:

| Product | Printful Cost | Recommended Retail | Margin |
|---------|--------------|-------------------|--------|
| Poster 12x18" | $8.50 | $24.99 | 66% |
| Canvas 12x16" | $16.00 | $44.99 | 64% |
| T-shirt | $9.50 | $29.99 | 68% |
| Hoodie | $22.00 | $59.99 | 63% |
| Mug | $6.50 | $18.99 | 66% |
| Phone case | $8.50 | $24.99 | 66% |

### Step 6: Order Samples (Mandatory)

- Go to any product > Order Sample
- Printful gives 20% discount on samples (up to 3 per month on free plan)
- Order at least one of every product type you plan to sell
- Check: color accuracy, print sharpness, material quality, packaging
- Use sample products for your own product photography

### Step 7: Set Up Shipping Profiles

- Printful calculates shipping automatically based on destination
- In your Shopify store, make sure shipping rates match or use "calculated at checkout"
- Consider offering free shipping on orders over $50 (build shipping cost into product price)
- Typical Printful shipping rates:
  - US: $4-8 for first item, $1-2 for additional
  - International: $8-15 for first item

## 7. Recommended File Specs for Art

### Art Prints / Posters

- **Resolution:** 300 DPI at final print size
- **Color mode:** CMYK for offset printing, sRGB acceptable for most POD
- **Bleed:** Add 0.25" (0.635 cm) on all sides beyond the trim line
- **Format:** PNG (preferred) or high-quality JPEG
- **File size:** Expect 30-100MB per file at full resolution
- **Example:** 18x24" print = 5400x7200px at 300 DPI + bleed = 5550x7350px

### Canvas Wraps

- **Resolution:** 300 DPI at final print size
- **Edge wrap:** Add 1.5" (3.81 cm) on all sides for gallery wrap. This area wraps around the wooden frame -- avoid critical details here.
- **Format:** PNG
- **Example:** 16x20" canvas = 4800x6000px at 300 DPI + 900px wrap on each side = 5700x6900px total

### Phone Cases

- **Resolution:** 300 DPI
- **Format:** PNG
- **Template:** Always download the specific template from your POD provider. Dimensions vary by phone model.
- **Typical safe area:** ~1200x2000px for the visible face, but the full template is larger to wrap edges

### Mugs (11oz Standard)

- **Resolution:** 300 DPI
- **Dimensions:** Wraparound template typically 2000x850px (may vary by provider)
- **Format:** PNG
- **Safe area:** Keep important elements away from the handle area (top/bottom 50px)

### T-Shirts

- **Resolution:** 300 DPI
- **Dimensions:** 4500x5400px (standard print area for Printful)
- **Format:** PNG with transparent background
- **Color note:** Use sRGB color space. Bright colors reproduce well on DTG (direct-to-garment) printing. Very dark designs look best on light shirts and vice versa.

### Hoodies

- **Resolution:** 300 DPI
- **Dimensions:** Same as t-shirts -- 4500x5400px for front print
- **Format:** PNG with transparent background
- **Notes:** Print area on hoodies is slightly smaller than t-shirts due to the front pocket. Check the template.

### Greeting Cards

- **Resolution:** 300 DPI
- **Dimensions:** 5x7" folded = 10x7" flat (3000x2100px at 300 DPI)
- **Bleed:** 0.125" on all sides
- **Format:** PDF (preferred for print shops) or PNG

### Stickers

- **Resolution:** 300 DPI minimum, 600 DPI for very small stickers
- **Format:** PNG with transparent background for die-cut
- **Bleed:** 0.0625" (1/16 inch) around the cut line
- **Tip:** Include a thin white border (0.04-0.08") around your design for a clean cut edge

### General Tips

- Always keep your source files in the highest resolution possible (vector if applicable)
- Export for each product from your source file rather than upscaling
- Maintain a master PSD/AI file and export layers per product
- Name files consistently: `design-name_product-type_dimensions.png`

## 8. Pricing Strategy

### Print-on-Demand Products

**Target: 2.5-3x production cost**

This covers the base cost, shipping subsidy, payment processing fees (~3%), and leaves healthy profit.

| Scenario | Base Cost | Retail Price | Your Profit (after ~3% fees) |
|----------|-----------|-------------|------------------------------|
| Low end (2.5x) | $10.00 | $25.00 | ~$14.25 |
| Sweet spot (2.75x) | $10.00 | $27.50 | ~$16.68 |
| Premium (3x) | $10.00 | $30.00 | ~$19.10 |

### Custom/Bulk Manufactured Products

**Target: 3-5x production cost**

Higher markup justified because you carry inventory risk and have upfront investment.

| Product | Bulk Cost | Retail Price | Multiplier | Margin |
|---------|-----------|-------------|------------|--------|
| T-shirt (bulk) | $6.00 | $32.99 | 5.5x | 82% |
| Phone case (Alibaba) | $3.00 | $24.99 | 8.3x | 88% |
| Crochet doll (India) | $8.00 | $59.99 | 7.5x | 87% |
| Greeting card | $0.60 | $5.99 | 10x | 90% |

### Digital Products

**Price for perceived value, not cost (cost is essentially $0)**

| Product | Suggested Price | Notes |
|---------|----------------|-------|
| Single wallpaper | $1.99-2.99 | Low price to maximize volume |
| Wallpaper pack (10) | $4.99 | Bundle discount drives volume |
| High-res art download | $8.99-14.99 | Premium positioning |
| Art bundle (5 pieces) | $24.99 | 30% off individual pricing |
| Coloring pages (pack) | $4.99-7.99 | Targets parents |
| WhatsApp sticker pack | $1.99-3.99 | Impulse price point |

### Limited Editions

**Target: 3-5x the regular version price**

- Announce limited quantities (e.g., "only 50 signed prints")
- Include certificate of authenticity
- Higher quality materials (museum-grade paper, archival inks)
- Hand-signed by the artist
- Example: Regular poster $24.99, Signed limited edition giclée $89.99-149.99

### Bundles

**Offer 15-20% discount versus buying items separately**

| Bundle Example | Individual Total | Bundle Price | Discount |
|---------------|-----------------|-------------|----------|
| 3-poster set | $74.97 | $59.99 | 20% |

| Full art collection (digital) | $89.91 | $69.99 | 22% |
| Kids book + crochet doll | $76.98 | $64.99 | 16% |

### Pricing by Growth Stage

| Stage | Strategy | Focus |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Launch (0-100 sales) | Competitive POD pricing, 2.5x multiplier | Build reviews, prove demand |
| Growing (100-500 sales) | Raise to 2.75-3x, add limited editions | Improve margins, test premium |

## 9. Quality Control Checklist

Complete this checklist before listing ANY new product for sale.

### Pre-Launch Checklist

- [ ] **Order a physical sample** of every product type before listing
- [ ] **Color accuracy:** Compare printed sample side-by-side with your screen (calibrated monitor)
  - If colors are off, adjust your file's saturation/brightness and reorder
  - Ask provider about their color profile (ICC profile) for precise matching
- [ ] **Print sharpness:** Check that fine details are crisp, text is readable, no pixelation
- [ ] **Material quality:** Feel the fabric, paper weight, case rigidity -- does it match the price point?
- [ ] **Durability testing:**
  - Apparel: Wash 3 times on normal cycle, check for fading, shrinkage, print cracking
  - Phone cases: Drop test from 3-4 feet onto hard surface
  - Mugs: Run through dishwasher 5 times
  - Stickers: Apply to a surface, leave for 1 week, check adhesion and color fade
  - Canvas: Check for warping, staple quality on back
- [ ] **Packaging inspection:** Note how the product arrives -- is it protected? Presentable?
- [ ] **Photography:** Take professional photos of the physical sample for product listings
  - Natural lighting, clean background
  - Show scale (hold in hand, place next to common objects)
  - Capture details and texture close-ups
- [ ] **Size verification:** Measure physical dimensions against listed specifications
- [ ] **Compare providers:** If using multiple POD services, order the same product from 2-3 and compare

### Ongoing Quality Control

- [ ] Re-order samples every 3-6 months to check for consistency
- [ ] Monitor customer reviews for quality complaints
- [ ] Address any quality issues with provider within 48 hours
- [ ] Keep a log of quality issues per provider (track in spreadsheet)
- [ ] Switch providers if quality drops below acceptable standard

### For Bulk/Custom Orders

- [ ] Request a pre-production sample before approving a full production run
- [ ] Inspect 10-20% of received inventory randomly
- [ ] Photograph any defective units and file claims with supplier
- [ ] Set a defect tolerance: typical standard is less than 3% defect rate

## 10. Shipping Partners (for Non-POD Products)

When you fulfill orders yourself (bulk inventory, signed editions, crochet dolls), you need shipping infrastructure.

### ShipStation (Multi-Carrier Management)

- **Website:** https://www.shipstation.com
- **Best for:** Managing shipping across multiple carriers from one dashboard
- **Pricing:** Starts at $9.99/mo (up to 25 shipments/month), $29.99/mo for 100+ shipments
- **Features:** Imports orders from Shopify/Etsy/Amazon, rate comparison, label printing, tracking
- **Carriers:** USPS, UPS, FedEx, DHL, and more
- **Setup difficulty:** Medium -- requires initial configuration of shipping rules
- **When to use:** When you ship 25+ non-POD orders per month

### Pirate Ship (Cheapest USPS/UPS Rates)

- **Website:** https://www.pirateship.com
- **Best for:** Cheapest domestic US shipping rates, zero monthly fees
- **Pricing:** Free platform. You pay only for postage at discounted commercial rates.
- **Carriers:** USPS and UPS
- **Example rates (domestic US, approximate):**
  - First-Class Package (under 1 lb): $3.50-5.00
  - Priority Mail (1-3 lbs): $8.00-12.00
  - Priority Mail Flat Rate Small Box: ~$9.00
- **Integration:** Shopify, Etsy, or manual CSV import
- **Setup difficulty:** Very easy (15 minutes)
- **When to use:** Starting out with any self-fulfilled US orders. Best value for domestic shipping.

### Easyship (International Shipping)

- **Website:** https://www.easyship.com
- **Best for:** International orders -- automatically calculates duties, taxes, and landed cost
- **Pricing:** Free plan (50 shipments/month), paid plans from $29/mo
- **Features:**
  - Compares 250+ courier services worldwide
  - Pre-calculates duties and taxes at checkout (no surprise charges for customers)
  - Generates customs documentation
  - Dashboard for tracking all international shipments
- **Key advantage for SikhiThreads:** Sikh diaspora is global. Easyship makes shipping to India, UK, Canada, Australia, and Singapore straightforward with full cost transparency.
- **Setup difficulty:** Medium -- integrates with Shopify, requires initial setup of product weights and dimensions

### DHL eCommerce

- **Website:** https://www.dhl.com/ecommerce
- **Best for:** Shipping to India and South/Southeast Asia
- **Pricing:** Varies by weight and destination; typically 20-40% cheaper than FedEx/UPS for Asia-bound packages
- **Typical transit time to India:** 7-14 business days
- **Access:** Sign up through Easyship or ShipStation for discounted rates, or contact DHL directly for a business account
- **Setup difficulty:** Medium -- requires business account setup

### India Post (Domestic India)

- **Website:** https://www.indiapost.gov.in
- **Best for:** Cheapest domestic shipping within India
- **Pricing:**
  - Speed Post (1-3 days): INR 35-80 for up to 500g
  - Registered Parcel (3-7 days): INR 25-50 for up to 500g
- **Tracking:** Available for Speed Post and Registered Post
- **When to use:** If you have Indian inventory (manufactured via IndiaMart suppliers) shipping to Indian customers
- **Setup difficulty:** Easy -- visit local post office or use online booking

### Shipping Partners by Growth Stage

| Stage | Monthly Volume | Recommended Setup |
|-------|---------------|-------------------|
| Starting (0-25 orders) | Under 25 | Pirate Ship for US, Easyship for international |
| Growing (25-100 orders) | 25-100 | ShipStation + Pirate Ship rates + Easyship |
| Scaling (100+ orders) | 100+ | ShipStation + DHL business account + consider 3PL |

### Third-Party Logistics (3PL) -- For Future Scaling

When you exceed 200+ self-fulfilled orders per month, consider a 3PL warehouse:

- **ShipBob** (https://www.shipbob.com) -- US, Canada, EU, Australia fulfillment
- **ShipMonk** (https://www.shipmonk.com) -- US-based, good for small items
- **Pricing:** ~$3-5 per order pick-and-pack + storage fees
- **Benefit:** They store your inventory, pick, pack, and ship. You focus on marketing and design.
