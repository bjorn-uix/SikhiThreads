---

## STEP 1: Shopify Account & Plan

1. Go to shopify.com → Start free trial (3 days free, then $1/month for first 3 months on Basic plan)
2. **Plan:** Start with Basic Shopify ($39/month after trial) — upgrade later if needed
3. **Domain:** Buy sikhithreads.com through Shopify ($14/year) or connect if already purchased

## STEP 2: Theme Selection

**Recommended theme:** **Dawn** (free) or **Sense** (free)
- Both are clean, image-focused, and work perfectly for art/visual brands
- Mobile-optimized (critical — 80%+ of your traffic will be mobile from Instagram)

**If budget allows ($300-400):** Consider **Prestige** or **Impulse** premium themes — better product showcase

## STEP 4: Print-on-Demand Setup

### Provider: **Printful** (recommended to start)
1. Create Printful account (free)
2. Install Printful app from Shopify App Store
3. Connect to your Shopify store
4. Upload your designs → create products → they auto-sync to Shopify
5. When a customer orders, Printful prints and ships directly

### Alternative: **Gelato** (better for international shipping)
- If significant audience is in India/UK/Canada, Gelato has local print facilities
- Can use both Printful + Gelato for different products

### Quality Control:
- **Order samples of EVERY product before going live**
- Check print quality, colors, packaging
- Use samples for product photography and Instagram content
- Budget: ~$200-300 for full sample order

---

## STEP 5: Digital Products Setup

### Use: **Shopify Digital Downloads** app (free) or **SendOwl**
1. Install the app
2. Upload digital files (wallpapers as ZIP, high-res art as PNG/JPG)
3. Set as "digital product" — no shipping needed
4. Customer gets instant download link after purchase

### File Preparation:
- Wallpapers: 1080x1920 (phone), 2560x1440 (desktop), 2048x2048 (iPad)
- Art downloads: 300 DPI, minimum 4000px on longest side
- Include a README in ZIP with thank you message + usage terms

## STEP 8: Payment & Shipping

### Payments:
- **Shopify Payments** (Stripe-powered) — default, lowest fees
- Add **PayPal** as alternative
- If selling to India: Add **Razorpay** or **UPI** options

### Shipping:
- Printful/Gelato handle shipping automatically
- Display estimated delivery times on product pages:
  - USA: 5-8 business days
  - Canada/UK: 7-12 business days
  - India: 10-18 business days
  - Rest of world: 10-20 business days
- **Free shipping threshold:** Free shipping on orders over $50 (increases average order value)

### Returns Policy:
- Since POD is made-to-order, standard policy is:
  - Damaged/defective items: Full replacement
  - Wrong item: Full replacement
  - Change of mind: No returns on custom-printed items (industry standard)
  - Digital products: No refunds after download

## PRODUCT PAGE TEMPLATE

Each product page should include:

**Title:** Descriptive + emotional (e.g., "Langar: Everyone is Equal — Art Print")

**Description format:**
```
[Opening line — emotional/cultural connection]

This piece depicts [scene description]. In Sikhi, [cultural significance of the scene].

Every SikhiThreads piece is created to bring the warmth and beauty of Sikhi into your home.

Details:
- [Size options]
- [Material/paper quality]
- [Framing options if applicable]
- [Shipping estimate]

Perfect for: [gifting occasions, room suggestions]
```

**Images:**
- Main product image (mockup from Printful)
- Lifestyle mockup (product on wall, in room)
- Close-up detail shot
- Size comparison (if applicable)

**Pricing strategy:**
- Price in odd numbers ($25, $35, $65) — feels less calculated
- Always show the "original" price crossed out if running a sale
- Bundle discounts: "Buy 2+ prints, get 10% off"
