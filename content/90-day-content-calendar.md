## Content Production Notes

### Reel Format (What works for SikhiThreads):
- 15–30 seconds sweet spot
- Slow pan/zoom through crochet scenes
- Emotional Gurbani or Punjabi music as audio
- Subtitles with the message/story
- End with a thought-provoking line or Gurbani quote

### Carousel Format:
- 5–7 slides
- First slide: bold hook
- Middle slides: value/story
- Last slide: CTA (save, share, shop)
- Consistent brand colors and typography

### Stories Strategy:
- Morning: Gurbani quote in crochet aesthetic
- Midday: Behind the scenes / poll / question
- Evening: Product highlight or community feature
- Night: Peaceful crochet scene + "Good night, Sangat"
