## KEY ASSUMPTIONS & RISKS

### Assumptions:
1. Follower growth continues at current trajectory (organic + light paid)
2. Store conversion rate: 1-3% of visitors (industry average for IG-driven stores)
3. Average order value grows as product line expands
4. Brand deals close at projected follower milestones
5. Children's book launches on schedule in June

### Risks & Mitigations:
| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Growth slows | Revenue targets missed | Increase ad spend, expand to TikTok/YouTube aggressively |
| Low conversion rate | Store underperforms | A/B test pricing, improve product photos, add urgency |
| Brand deals don't close | Miss $4-6K/month stream | Focus on direct product sales (higher margin anyway) |
| Children's book delayed | Miss June revenue | Pre-orders de-risk; launch in July instead |
| POD quality issues | Returns, bad reviews | Order samples first, switch providers if needed |
